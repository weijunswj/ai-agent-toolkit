- [ ] Pending - Confirm repository and branch.
- [ ] Pending - Confirm Dockerfile, buildpack, or build path.
- [ ] Pending - Confirm app healthcheck endpoint.
- [ ] Pending - Confirm required env vars without printing secret values.
- [ ] Pending - Confirm persistent volumes and backup expectations.
- [ ] Pending - Confirm public/private exposure; keep databases, caches, and admin ports private by default.
- [!] Needs owner approval - Approve custom domain, SSL, DNS, production env vars, and production deploy target.
- [ ] Pending - Confirm no secrets are committed.
- [ ] Pending - Deploy after approval.
- [ ] Pending - Run healthcheck and inspect logs without printing secrets.
- [ ] Pending - Record rollback plan and deployment evidence report.
